
##Chapter Two: Key Takeaways

This chapter focuses on the essential skills for bond transaction calculations and data manipulation, particularly with Python's Pandas library.

**Core Messages:**

* **Accrued Interest:** Crucial for accurate bond transaction pricing.  
* **Date Manipulation:** A fundamental skill for financial data.  
* **Pandas `apply` Method:** An efficient way to enhance DataFrames with new columns.  
* **Excel Integration:** Convert DataFrames to Excel for easy sharing and accessibility.

**Applications:**

The chapter includes eight applications designed to reinforce these concepts:

* **Date Manipulation (5 applications):** Practice and solidify your ability to work with dates.  
* **Pandas Skills (2 applications):** Develop proficiency in using Pandas for data tasks.  
* **Transaction Price Calculation (1 application):** Apply your knowledge to determine the transaction price of a coupon bond.

**Chapter Exercise:**
