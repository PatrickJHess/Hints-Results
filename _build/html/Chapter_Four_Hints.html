Application: Is the payoff matrix full column rank?#

Settlement date January 21\(^{st}\) 2025 and semi-annual coupons.

Maturity

Coupon

   2.00

   July 31\(^{st}\) 2027

   1.00

   4.00

Notice that the July 31\(^{st}\) 2027 bond is now a coupon bond with five payment dates. Confirm this with the unique_pay_dates. The rank is the minimum of the number of columns or rows. Four bonds is not enough to estimate eight zero prices.

Chapter Exercise#

Using the term structure calculated with 304 bonds

Calculate the forward rates for the following dates.

Start Date

End Date

   January 28\(^{th}\) 2025

   February 2\(^{nd}\) 2026

   February 2\(^{nd}\) 2026

   February 1\(^{st}\) 2027

   February 1\(^{st}\) 2027

   February 1\(^{st}\) 2028

   January 31\(^{st}\) 2028

   January 31\(^{st}\) 2030

  1. Download Excel workbook from Dropbox

  2. Create the transaction prices with accrued_interest function

  3. Create the payoff matrix with create_payoff_matrix.

  4. Estimate the zero prices with lsqt function of linalg module of NumPy.

  5. Create a list of unique dates sequential lists from the start and end dates.

  6. Locate the zero prices for the unique dates and calculate forward rates as the negative of the difference in the natural log of zero prices.
